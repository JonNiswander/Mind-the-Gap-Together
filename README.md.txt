# Mind-the-Gap-Together
Mind the Gap Together Client Portal
### Pinellas County Community Resource Hub

A free, searchable web resource connecting Pinellas County residents 
with local services including housing, healthcare, food, jobs, and more.

## About
Mind the Gap Together is a nonprofit organization dedicated to bridging 
the gap between residents in need and the community resources available 
to them across Pinellas County, Florida.

## What This Site Does
- Search hundreds of local resources by keyword
- Find food pantries open today
- Locate emergency housing and shelters
- Access free healthcare and mental health services
- Find job listings and career resources
- Connect with volunteer opportunities
- Donate to support the mission

## Tech
Single-page HTML/CSS/JS application. No frameworks, no dependencies, 
no build step required.
